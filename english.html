Shenhe

A genshin discord bot: damage calculator, resin reminder, material calculator, todo lists, character builds, artifact display, character showcase, wish analysis

Character showcase

No need to register or use a scanner, just enter your UID and view your character artifacts, weapons, and talents! You can also use the damage calculator, which considers team, artifacts, and weapon buffs automatically!

Realtime notes

View your resin, serenitea pot, and expedition status in realtime, straight from discord!

Notification features

Never see 160/160 again! You will get notified in discord after your resin exceeds a certain amount you set. You can also use the talent notification feature, which notifies you when it's time to farm items for a character.

Todo list

Like to pre-farm items? Shenhe helps you calculate the amount of materials needed for upgrading characters and weapons, then add them directly to your personal todo list!

Localization

Shenhe is built in Traditional Chinese, but she can speak English too!
